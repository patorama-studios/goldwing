# Goldwing Association Member Guide

## Logging in
- Visit `/login.php` and sign in with your member credentials.

## Dashboard
- See your membership number, type, status, expiry date, and linked associates.
- Quick actions link to profile edits, billing, and history.

## Profile
- Update contact details, directory privacy level, and assistance flags.
- Chapter changes require approval from the committee.

## Wings Magazine
- View the latest issue and access the archive.

## Notice Board
- Read pinned and recent notices from the association.

## Events
- Upcoming events are filtered by your chapter or include all-chapter events.

## Billing
- View payment history and renewal status.
- Renewals are paid via Stripe checkout links sent by email.

## Membership Application
- New members can apply at `/apply.php`.
- After approval, payment links are emailed to activate membership.
